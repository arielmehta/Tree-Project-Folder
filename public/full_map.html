Our Trees

Below is the site for the project, located behind the Masonic Home for the elderly, and bordering the Garin Regional Wilderness. The different colored icons show the locations of living trees by tree species. Using the data on this map, and data collected from the area, we hope to find areas most conducive to tree growth which will allow us to better learn where reforestation attempts will be most succesful. View the Key below for the different species of trees represented on the map. Click on the colored map markers to view more details about the trees.
