Our Trees

Below is the site for the project, located behind the Masonic Home for the elderly, and next to the Garin Regional Wilderness. The green tree icons show the locations of living trees while the brown ones represent trees that have died. Using the data on this map of tree health, and data collected from the area, we hope to find areas most conducive to tree growth which will allow us to better learn where reforestation attempts will be most succesful. View the Key below the map to find descriptions/explanations of the different colored areas on the map.
